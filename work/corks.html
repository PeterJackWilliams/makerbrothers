CORKS OF CARGO

Tasked with fitting out a new container in Wapping Wharf’s

Cargo development for Corks. As an addition to their two shops,

Corks wanted to open wine merchants with the opportunity

to drink on and off the premises, taking advantage of the dock

side location. Maker Brothers weretasked with fitting out the

container straight from its ply lining. With an industrial feel,

wine racks and counters created from reclaimed scaffold boards,

industrial style lighting and feature furniture.

All works were completed and fitted in less than 72 hours.
