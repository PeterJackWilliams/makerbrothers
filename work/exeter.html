Exeter - Uprising

EXETER

UPRISING

Maker Brothers were tasked with updating the bakery on Magdalene Rd

in Exeter. The old Bakery a favourite haunt for many, it was

important to maintain the atmosphere that the customers

loved so much. The design was undertaken by Shed Design and then

handed over to Maker Brothers. A new main counter and oak furntiture

was produced in our workshop prior to install, the site work included

a total rip out, re-plastering, timber wall effects, ew electrics, plumbing,

tiling and re-decoration. The project was completed in 2 weeks.
