PENRYN

THE WAREHOUSE

Maker Brothers were contracted to carry out a full and comprehensive fit out of the Warehouse building in Penryn, Cornwall. Becoming Origin Coffee’s coffee shop in their home county. Maker Brothers worked alongside Hart Miller Design to transform this challenging piece of old Cornwall into a bright, open coffee space more akin to central London. With All furniture elements made by Maker Brothers in our Bristol base the finished project is a striking space.
